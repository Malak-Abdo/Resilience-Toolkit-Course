The 7 AM Protocol - Strategic Decision Scripts

When a shock hits, your staff should not wait for you to wake up or finish a meeting.

They should have a pre-authorized script to begin the "Adaptive Pivot."

In Case of Logistics Delay (e.g., food shipment arrival)

📌 Use this when: Trucks are delayed but beneficiaries are already prepared to move to the distribution site.

🔄 The Reframe: Move from "Wait for the truck" to "Manage the Expectation."

⏰ If the supply chain has shifted by 72 hours:

"Our mission today is not food distribution; our mission today is to deliver information to beneficiaries and notify them. Start contacting community leaders now."

⚠️ Crisis 3 of 4 - Data Breach

"A laptop containing sensitive beneficiary data is stolen from a staff member's car. The staff member is terrified of being fired. What is the best course of action?"

○
Fire the staff member immediately. They broke the security rules.
○
Report the incident to security and the donor immediately. Secure all accounts, and support the staff member. Focus on fixing the issue rather than punishing the honesty.
○
Keep the incident quiet. Do not report it to anyone and hope that the stolen data is never accessed.
